Cinnamon Eggy Bread with stewed apples

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 apples
  • Butter 10 g
  • 3 eggs
  • Milk 4 tablespoons
  • Sugar 6 tablespoons
  • Powdered cinnamon 1 teaspoon
  • Icing sugar

Instructions

  1. Start off with slicing slightly stale bread, 4-5 days old.
  2. Cut the apples, keep the skin on
  3. Get your pan nice and hot, then, sprinkle 2 tbsp of sugar and cook until light brown.
  4. Place the apples into the pan and add 50g of butter
  5. Once you've glazed the apples, add 2 tbsp of water into the pan. Bring it to boil and cook for 10 mins.
  6. Whisk 3 whole eggs, add 4tbsp of milk and 3 tbsp of sugar. Add a tsp of powdered cinnamon. Whisk the mixture nicely.
  7. Get another pan, get it nice and hot and add some oil. Add 50gms of butter
  8. Dip the bread into the egg mixture and place on the pan. Cook until nicely colored.
  9. Put your bread onto the plate and place the apples on top. Finish with a touch of Icing sugar

Bon appetit!
